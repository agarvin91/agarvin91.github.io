About Angela

Angela is a poet and a picture book author who feels perfectly suited to pour story into that inquisitive 3 to 8 year old. She believes important truths can be depicted in rhyme to make them more memorable and enjoyable for today’s children. It also makes reading more fun for the adult in the room. Angela won her first rhyming contest in 3rd grade and doesn’t plan to stop anytime soon. Her brain in refrain can’t be detained!

While pouring into her 3 grown sons and 5 grandchildren, Angela also has had the privilege of pouring Bible truths into elementary children for the past 15 years from stage at church, at camps, and at Good News Clubs in local public schools.She feels there is no greater calling than to equip and encourage the children of today for all their tomorrows. She hopes you will see her heart for children and her heart for Jesus in her manuscripts.
